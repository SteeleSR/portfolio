About me

From sales to software...

I'm Sam, I'm based in the South West and I'm training to become a full stack developer. I enjoy travelling, sports, cooking, quizzes and being within 100 metres of a dog.

Despite a successful stint within the property sector, I was lacking fulfillment and mental stimulation. During the pandemic, I began teaching myself the basics of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP and became addicted to the process. Software development provided me with the continuous learning and creative outlet that I was craving.

Since discovering my programming passion, I have recently begun the Full Stack Track with award-winning iO Academy in Bath. This immersive 16 week course shall provide me with a solid foundation from which to specialise further. Afterwards, I hope to combine my recently obtained knowledge with the skills from my previous career to become a valuable member of a software development team.
